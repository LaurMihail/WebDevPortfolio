The Best Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Star Wars series

One of the most successful film series of all time. The movies relies on the strength of pure narrative, in the most basic storytelling form known to man, the Journey.

The Godfather

The story views the Mafia from the inside. That is its secret, its charm, its spell; in a way, it has shaped the public perception of the Mafia ever since.

Indiana Jones series

The hat, the whip, the theme song. Harrison Ford stars as the iconic adventurer Indiana Jones, a swashbuckling archaeology professor who spends his free time tracking down lost artifacts in mythic locations.
